Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. Fill in your details including name, email, and password. Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox, and your account will be ready to use.

After logging in, navigate to your dashboard and click on "Sell an Item". Fill in the item details including title, description, price, and category. Upload clear photos of your item, and click "List Item" to publish your listing on the marketplace.

EasyTrade supports various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption to ensure your financial information remains safe.

Our buyer protection policy ensures that you receive the item as described or get your money back. If an item doesn't arrive or is significantly different from the description, you can open a dispute within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also view the tracking information in your order history on your account dashboard. Click on the specific order to see detailed tracking information and estimated delivery dates.

Most items on EasyTrade can be returned within 14 days of delivery if they are in their original condition. Some categories may have different return policies, which will be clearly stated on the product listing. Return shipping costs may apply depending on the reason for the return.

You can contact a seller by clicking on the "Contact Seller" button on their product listing page. This will open a messaging interface where you can ask questions about the item. All communications are kept within the EasyTrade platform for security and record-keeping purposes.

EasyTrade charges a small commission fee of 5% on each successful sale. There are no listing fees or monthly subscription costs. The commission is automatically deducted from the final sale price when the transaction is completed, so you only pay when you sell.

To change your password or update account information, log in to your account and click on your profile icon in the top right corner. Select "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu. From there, you can update your personal information, change your password, manage notification preferences, and update payment methods.

If you receive a damaged item, take clear photos of the damage and contact the seller immediately through the EasyTrade messaging system. If the seller doesn't respond within 48 hours or doesn't offer a satisfactory solution, you can open a dispute through our Resolution Center. Make sure to initiate this process within 7 days of receiving the item for the best outcome.
